Dr. D. Kashinatham

Dr. D. Kashinatham is a Consultant Urologist, Andrologist & Transplant Surgeon at Yashoda Hospitals, Malakpet. He completed his MBBS from Guntur Medical College in 1999, followed by an MS in General Surgery from PGIMER, Chandigarh, in 2002, and an MCh in Urology from SVIMS, Tirupati, in 2007. He specializes in Laparoscopic Surgeries and has performed over 500 Kidney Transplant Surgeries.

Education Qualifications

  • 2002: MS (General Surgery), Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education (PGIMER), Chandigarh
  • 1999: MBBS, Guntur Medical College, NTR University of Health Sciences
