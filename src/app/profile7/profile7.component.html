Dr. Aman Chandra Deshpande

Dr. Aman Chandra Deshpande is a Consultant Urologist, Andrologist, Kidney Transplant, and Robotic Surgeon at Yashoda Hospitals, Malakpet. He has extensive knowledge and experience in treating all types of urological diseases with the utmost proficiency. He specializes in Advanced Endourology, Laparoscopic Urology, Reconstructive Urology, Andrology, Urogynaecology, Kidney Transplantation, and Robotic Surgery and is well-versed in both Modern and Conventional Medicine.
