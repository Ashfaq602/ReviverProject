Dr. Karthika Reddy

Dr. Karthika Reddy is a Consultant Clinical and Interventional Pulmonologist at Yashoda Hospitals, Hitec City, with over 8 years of experience. She has a keen interest in Interventional Pulmonology and specializes in the treatment and management of Pulmonary Infections, Interstitial Lung Diseases, Severe Asthma, and COPD, among others.

Education Qualifications

  • 2019-2020: Fellowship in Interventional Pulmonology, Yashoda Hospitals
  • 2017: SCE Respiratory Medicine, Royal College of Physicians, UK
  • 2014-2017: DNB Pulmonary Medicine, P. D. Hinduja National Hospital & Medical Research Centre, Mumbai
  • 2006-2011: MBBS, SVS Medical College, Mahabubnagar

Services offered

  • Pulmonology Outpatient and Inpatient Services
  • Advanced Pulmonary Function Tests
  • Advanced Sleep Diagnostics
  • Diagnostic Bronchoscopy
  • Medical Thoracoscopy
