Our Vision


Over 3 decades of experience

A vision to create a world-class integrated healthcare delivery system in India, entailing the finest medical skills combined with compassionate care gave birth to Omega Hospitals in July 2010. When a few well-known, dedicated Oncologists with a zeal to provide exceptional patient care came together, they realized the lack of comprehensive care for cancer patients in India. After many brainstorming sessions with one aim to start a leading cancer treatment center and care provider in the world, Dr. Ch. Mohana Vamsy, Chief Surgical Oncologist, and his colleagues wanted to ensure that India gets its own affordable cancer care for all sections of its society.
